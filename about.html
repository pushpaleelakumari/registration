About Us

What is acciojob?

Gurgaon, Haryana based AccioJob is a venture by IIT Delhi Alumni and backed by Y Combinator. They strive to make the youth Industry Ready via our Certification based Internship programs and Job Guaranteed Courses. They provide hands-on training, mentoring, and live project support to engage in a transformational learning experience.
